Southern Roleplay

Welcome to Southern Roleplay

Southern Roleplay is a New Zealand–based FiveM roleplay community set around Christchurch, Canterbury, Amberley, and Woodend. We focus on realistic emergency services roleplay with NZ-style Police, FENZ, and CAS, as well as civilian roleplay and community events.

What the Server is About

  • Realistic New Zealand emergency services roleplay.
  • Friendly, community-focused environment.
  • Serious roleplay with common sense and fun.
  • Active staff team and regular updates.

Server Rules

These are example rules – you can edit them in the HTML to match your exact server rules.

  1. No RDM (Random Deathmatch) or VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch).
  2. No Fail RP – always act realistically for your character and situation.
  3. No combat logging or disconnecting to avoid RP or punishment.
  4. Respect staff decisions and follow all staff instructions.
  5. No cheating, modding, or exploiting bugs/glitches.
  6. Use common sense and keep things respectful in voice and text chat.
  7. Do not advertise other communities or servers.
  8. Read the full rule list in the Discord for detailed rules & punishments.

FiveM / Server Rules

Please make sure you have read and agreed to the rules before joining.

  1. Follow all FiveM Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  2. Use a proper RP name – no troll or offensive names.
  3. Mic is strongly recommended for most RP scenarios.
  4. Listen to staff and respond to administrative messages quickly.
  5. Any form of cheating, mod menu, or unfair advantage is a permanent ban.
  6. Read the full, up-to-date rule list in the Discord server.
